Vertigo River Expeditions

Our mission is to deliver world-class, safety-first whitewater rafting experiences that blend adrenaline-pumping adventure with environmental stewardship. Through expert guiding, premium gear, and a passion for the outdoors, we transform curious travelers into lifelong river advocates, one rapid at a time.

History

Vertigo River Expeditions wasn't born in a boardroom; it was forged in the freezing, churning waters of the Lochsa River. In the summer of 2012, lifelong friends and seasoned river guides, Liam "Mac" Mackenzie and Elena Vance, found themselves uninspired by the commercialized, "conveyor-belt" style of mainstream rafting companies. They felt the industry had lost its connection to the raw, educational, and deeply transformative power of the river.
